# TravelLyns
Transform interests into structured projects and learning journeys.
TravelLyns is an AI-powered travel planning workspace for busy professionals who want a high-confidence trip plan fast—without juggling dozens of tabs, spreadsheets, and conflicting recommendations.

**Problem:** Planning a short PTO trip is time-consuming and mentally exhausting. Travelers must compare options across many sites, estimate costs, and build schedules that are *actually doable*. Price volatility and budget sensitivity make it even harder to choose confidently, and many itineraries end up overpacked or unrealistic once transit time and pacing are considered.

**Target audience:** Busy professionals taking short trips (typically 3–7 days) who need an efficient, trustworthy plan within clear constraints (dates, budget, interests, and time/energy).

**Proposed solution:** TravelLyns turns a few inputs—destination, travel dates, budget, and interests—into a structured itinerary with:
- **Timed activity blocks** and realistic day pacing
- **Transit/time assumptions** to keep plans feasible
- A per-day **“stress score”** to indicate how rushed or relaxed the schedule is
- A **budget breakdown** so users can understand expected costs at a glance

**MVP demo scenario (end-to-end):** A user enters destination + dates + budget + interests and receives a 3–5 day itinerary with timed blocks, transit assumptions, a stress score per day, and an estimated cost breakdown—all in a single organized workspace.

**Expected impact:** TravelLyns reduces decision fatigue and planning time, helps travelers avoid unrealistic schedules, and improves confidence that a trip plan will fit both their budget and their available time—so they can spend less time planning and more time enjoying the trip.
